🚀 Exploring Beyond Boundaries

A nonfiction tribute by Sivaneswaran R. celebrating the brilliance and resilience of ISRO

📘 About the Book

*Exploring Beyond Boundaries* is a detailed and passionate tribute to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). As a young student and future innovator, I wanted to capture the legacy of ISRO—from humble beginnings to Mars missions and beyond.

The book features inspiring stories, timelines of major missions, glossary of key terms, acknowledgments of ISRO legends, and my own reflections on what space exploration means for young dreamers like me.

"You don't need to be born in a rocket lab to touch the stars. ISRO showed us that dreams built with courage and science can launch a nation." – Sivaneswaran R.

🚀 Chapters & Highlights

  • 🛰️ *From Bicycle Launches to PSLVs* – ISRO’s origin story
  • 🌍 *INSATs & Remote Sensing* – Watching over Earth with care
  • 🪐 *Mangalyaan* – The budget Mars mission that stunned the world
  • 💡 *Innovators Behind the Scenes* – Kalam, Sarabhai, and silent stars
  • 📖 *Glossary of Space Terms* – Making space science simple for everyone
  • 🎓 *My Reflections* – Why ISRO’s journey shaped my vision for the future

🧠 My Purpose in Writing

As someone deeply passionate about both science and storytelling, I wanted to create a book that would excite young minds the way ISRO excites mine. I believe it's not just a story of rockets—it's a story of India’s determination, brilliance, and belief in knowledge. I hope my book becomes a launchpad for other students who dare to dream beyond the clouds.
