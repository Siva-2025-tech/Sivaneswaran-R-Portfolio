🚘 Arduino EV Transmission System

🔋 Automatic Gear Shifter for Electric Vehicles

I designed an Arduino-powered automatic transmission system to optimize speed and torque distribution in electric cars. Using sensors and motor inputs, the system decides when to shift gears automatically—maximizing efficiency and reducing human error.

  • Programmed in C++ using Arduino IDE
  • Utilized servo motors for shifting logic
  • Tested via simulated gear profiles and drive cycles

🌱 Tree-Planting Robot

🤖 Environmental Robotics in Action

Built and coded through SP Robotic Works, this robot was designed to dig a hole, drop a sapling, and gently press soil back—all autonomously. I used it to plant a tree for A. Snehalkumar as part of a record-setting eco-event 🌍
