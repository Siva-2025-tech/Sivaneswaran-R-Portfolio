🚀 Shivam Aerospace

Founded by Sivaneswaran R. – Where Innovation Begins With Imagination

🛰️ Our Mission

Shivam Aerospace is a student-founded rocket initiative dedicated to creating sustainable, educational, and accessible space technologies. We design clean-fuel launch vehicles, robotics systems, and STEM programs for the future.

  • 🚀 Build rocket systems designed by and for students
  • 📡 Enable educational payloads and CubeSat deployment
  • 🧠 Combine aerospace with AI, automation, and Arduino systems
  • 🌱 Explore green propulsion and reusable designs

🔥 TTLSS E1: Valkyrie Sparrow

Rocket Class: Tactical Test Launch System – Experimental (E1)

  • 🚀 Lightweight, two-stage suborbital platform
  • ⚙️ Arduino-based flight computer with real-time telemetry
  • 📦 Carries up to 20kg—perfect for CubeSats and atmospheric sensors
  • 🌀 Liquid + solid fuel hybrid concept under development

“Precision from Earth, Velocity toward the Stars.”

🛠️ In Progress

  • 🔬 PulseDrive: Low-emission propulsion test unit (Arduino-controlled)
  • 🛰️ SkyLink EDU: Ground station kit for tracking real satellites
  • 📖 Shivam Space Journal: Monthly newsletter with mission logs & ideas

These are passion projects engineered at the intersection of science, storytelling, and sustainability.

🌍 Our Vision

We believe every student should have access to the stars. Shivam Aerospace is building:

  • 🚀 Accessible launch kits for student experiments
  • 🎓 Classroom-to-orbit outreach through online workshops
  • 🌌 A platform where kids can dream of space and code their way into orbit
