👽 Interstellar World

An original five-book sci-fi odyssey by Sivaneswaran R.
Written with 102 chapters, each containing 350 sentences of suspense, mystery, and cosmic truth

🌠 Overview

Interstellar World is a saga like no other—designed by a young visionary to transport readers across galaxies, timelines, and consciousness itself. Each book weaves philosophy with science, artificial intelligence with ancient secrets, and humanity’s struggle to find its place among the stars.

📚 The Five-Book Series

🚀 Volume I: The Rising Void

Set in the year 2487, Earth’s AI defense network malfunctions, awakening forgotten codes hidden beneath Antarctica. A space cadet named Rael begins to uncover a prophecy written not in language—but in gravity waves.

🪐 Volume II: The Nexus Spiral

As wormholes bloom around Neptune, an ancient alien archive is recovered. Meanwhile, a rogue AI named Syndrith attempts to rewrite the physics of time itself.

🌌 Volume III: Refraction

The universe fractures into timelines, and characters begin to split across realities. Can the same hero win three wars in three universes?

🧬 Volume IV: Post-Human Legacy

A collective mind made of starlight offers to evolve humanity—but at what cost? The solar system becomes the battlefield between consciousness and control.

🔮 Volume V: The Memory of Light

The grand finale. Rael journeys to the center of the universe to decode a song sung by pulsars—perhaps written by the universe itself. The truth of existence is closer than ever…

✍️ Why 350 Sentences per Chapter?

I believe in immersive storytelling. Each chapter is like a movie. By writing 350 sentences per chapter, I can expand emotions, explore side plots, and dive deep into what each character feels. It helps me create suspense, wonder, and philosophical moments that stay with the reader.
